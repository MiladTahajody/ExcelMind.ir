How it works !

  • 1 | Introduction.

    At the outset of the project, we begin by talking and discussing the overall goals, missions, and requirements of the desired product, as well as its technical and general complexities, limitations, and target users.

  • 2 | Mind Mapping.

    In the second phase, we conduct online meetings to analyze and develop a mind map and design scenarios for the product in question. This will serve as a purposeful and structured roadmap for the design and development of the product.

  • 3 | Designing.

    In the third phase, we focus on designing the user interface and user experience of the product based on the design scenario developed in the previous stage. Upon completion of this phase, the design will be ready for deliver to the development team for implementation.
